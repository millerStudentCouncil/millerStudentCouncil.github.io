Fancy Dance Cooking and Serving Volunteers 2014-15

This form is for 7th grade students who have taken Mrs. Reed's Cooking class before and would like to volunteer to help cook and/or serve food at the Fancy Dance on Friday, March 20, 2015. Dinner will take place from approximately 6-7 P.M., but if you are cooking, you should be available from after-school to 8 P.M. (serving is around 5-8), so please have your schedule free for that evening. Volunteers are greatly needed and appreciated!

*Please note: We will only assign you to one job, but please sign up for all of the jobs if you are comfortable doing/have time for. Volunteers are chosen based on what spots we need to be filled and how many people volunteer.*

Thank you,
Fancy Dance Committee
